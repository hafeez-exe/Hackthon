[VISUAL HOOK]
Most students freeze on 'tell me about yourself.'

[LINKEDIN CAPTION]
Here is why: You try to summarize your entire life story in 2 minutes. 

No recruiter wants that.

They don't want to hear about your high school grades or how much you 'love coding.' They want to know one thing: Can you do the job?

Use our Present-Past-Future structure:
1. Present: State your current major, primary skills, and active tech stacks.
2. Past: Tell them about 1-2 projects you actually shipped.
3. Future: Why this company matches your goal.

Keep it under 90 seconds. Simplicity wins.
